World Sex Map

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

In short: the World Sex Map collects no data, creates no accounts, makes no tracking requests, and requests no browser permissions. Everything runs locally in your browser.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There is no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party code.

Network requests

The app makes no tracking or third-party requests. All code, map outlines and country data are bundled with the app and loaded locally; once loaded it works fully offline.

Local storage

The app saves a couple of small flags in your browser's localStorage to remember that you've seen the one-time welcome tutorial and flat-map tip, so they don't show again. These never leave your device and you can clear them at any time through your browser settings.

Permissions

It is a static web page — it requests no permissions, has no backend, and runs entirely in your browser.

Content & intended audience

This is an educational data visualisation about human sexuality, aggregated at the country level from public research and surveys. It contains no explicit imagery, but it addresses adult themes and is intended for a general adult and educational audience. Figures are indicative survey estimates, not statements about any individual.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated date above will change with it.
