World Sex Map

An interactive atlas of human sexuality around the world — and a timeline of sexuality through history. Colour the globe by any perspective: how often people have sex, satisfaction, number of partners, age at first sex, sexual openness, acceptance of premarital sex and of homosexuality, infidelity, porn interest, contraception, condom use, HIV, teen births, the legal age of consent, or the legal status of sex work. Click any country for its full profile.

Below, scrub the history of sexuality — from ancient fertility cults and the Kama Sutra to the condom, the Pill, Kinsey, the sexual revolution, AIDS and marriage equality — and see where each milestone happened.

Please read — data quality varies a lot. Much of this is self-reported survey data (people under- and over-report sex), often dated and from non-representative samples. Behavioural figures (frequency, partners, satisfaction, infidelity) are the weakest; legal and health figures (age of consent, HIV, teen births, contraception) are the strongest. “Perceived attractiveness” is explicitly subjective — it reflects opinion polls and stereotypes, not any objective fact. Treat everything here as indicative, never as exact and never as a judgement of any individual.

Sources: DHS & MICS · UNAIDS · WHO · UN Population Division · World Bank · World Values Survey · Pew Research · Natsal · Kinsey Institute · Durex Global Sex Survey · Pornhub Insights · AgeOfConsent.net. Borders: Natural Earth. Globe: globe.gl.
