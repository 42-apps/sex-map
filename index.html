About the data

Figures are drawn from DHS & MICS surveys, UNAIDS, the WHO, the UN Population Division, the World Bank, the World Values Survey, Pew Research, Natsal, the Kinsey Institute, the Durex Global Sex Survey, Pornhub Insights and national statistics. Country outlines: Natural Earth (public domain). Globe: globe.gl.

Please read — data quality varies a lot. Much of this is self-reported survey data (people under- and over-report sex), often dated and from non-representative samples. Behavioural figures (frequency, partners, satisfaction, infidelity) are the weakest; legal and health figures (age of consent, HIV, teen births, contraception) are the strongest. “Perceived attractiveness” is explicitly subjective — it reflects opinion polls and stereotypes, not any objective fact. Treat everything here as indicative, never as exact, and never as a judgement of any individual.
